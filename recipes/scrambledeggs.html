Joshua's Famous Scrambled Eggs

Description:

Anybody can make eggs their way, but not many can make them the RIGHT way. I'll be presenting my fool-proof method to making eggs that'll have you craving breakfast for luncn and dinner. You're welcome

Ingredients

  • Eggs, 2 whole
  • Egg whites, 2 servings
  • Butter
  • Salt
  • Pepper

Steps:

  1. Combine and whisk together egg and egg-whites in a bowl/mug
  2. Pour contents into medium-heat pan
  3. Consistently push eggs around pan to produce even heat and prevent sticking
  4. Remove at desired consistency
