Joshua's Famous Scrambled Eggs

Description:

Anybody can make eggs their way, but not many can make them the RIGHT way. I'll be presenting my fool-proof method to making eggs that'll have you craving breakfast for lunch and dinner. You're welcome

Ingredients

  • Eggs, 2 whole
  • Egg whites, 2 servings
  • Butter, 1 tsp
  • Salt, 1-2 dashes
  • Pepper, 1-2 dashes

Steps:

  1. Combine and whisk together egg and egg-whites in a bowl/mug
  2. Pour contents into medium-heat pan, add butter at same time
  3. Consistently push eggs around pan to transfer even heat and prevent sticking
  4. Add salt and pepper just before eggs at desired consistency
  5. Remove eggs and enjoy!
