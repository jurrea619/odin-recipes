Fasting Drink

Description

I practice Intermittent Fasting daily, typically going at least 16 hours without food or calories of any kind. Combined with a workout at the tail-end, you can imagine how much I put my body through during my fasting period. I use this drink to replensish essential minerals to aid in keeping my body prepared getting deeper into my fast. Recovery from a workout is important, but so is preparation.

Ingredients

  • 2 Tbsp Apple Cider Vinegar
  • 12-16 oz Water
  • 1/4-1/2 tsp Cream of Tartar
  • 1 Lime/Lemon
  • 1/2 Tbsp Pink Sea Salt

Steps

  1. Combine all ingredients
  2. Shake or stir
  3. Enjoy!
