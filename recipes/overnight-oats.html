Overnight Oats

Description

A pleasantly sweet, rich grab-and-go snack for those who need a way to spice up their morning oats routine. By allowing the oats proper time to soak in a cold milk or milk substitute, we end up with a pudding like dish which serves as a blank canvas for toppings.

Ingredients

  • Oats, 0.5 cup
  • Soy Milk, 1/2-3/4 cup
  • Chia Seeds, 1 tbsp
  • Agave Nectar, 1 tbsp
  • Blueberries, 1/2 cup

Steps

  1. Stir together chia seeds, milk, and oats in a mason jar container
  2. Cover and store in refridgerator. Soak overnight (~8 hrs)
  3. Add toppings and sweetener, stir and enjoy!
