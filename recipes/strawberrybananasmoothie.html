Strawberry Banana Smoothie

Description:

A perfect smoothie that can be enjoyed anytime of the day. A nutritionally dense snack perfect post-workout for refueling tired muscles. Just the right fuel for your body

Ingredients:

  • Banana, 1 whole peeled
  • Strawberries, 5-6 whole frozen
  • Soy Milk, 1.5 cup
  • Water, 0.5
  • Chia seeds, 1 tbsp
  • Spinach, 1 cup
  • Pea Protein, 1 serving
  • Creatine, 1 tsp
  • Sweetener of choice, 1-2 tbsp

Steps:

  1. Toss all ingredients into your blender
  2. Blend until smooth
  3. Serve and enjoy
