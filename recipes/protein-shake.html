Strawberry Banana Protein Smoothie

Description

After fasting for around 16 hours, which ends with a grueling workout, nothing gets me more excited than powering down a delicious protein smoothie. Hard work in the gym needs recovery, but there's no need to sacrifice flavor when it comes to nutrition. This smoothie accomplishes this in spades.

Ingredients

  • 1 C Soy Milk
  • 1 Scoop Pea Protein
  • 1 Banana
  • 3-4 Whole Strawberries

Steps

  1. Combine all ingredients in a blender
  2. Toss in some ice cubes, blend for 10-15 seconds
  3. Enjoy!
